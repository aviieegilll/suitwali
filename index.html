Frequently Asked Questions

How long does made-to-order take? +

Most pieces are ready in 3–5 weeks depending on handwork complexity; bridal pieces may take longer. Exact timelines are confirmed on WhatsApp once details are shared.

Can I change the colour or fabric? +

Yes — every design shown is a starting template. Share your preferred palette and we will confirm available fabric options for that silhouette.

How are measurements taken? +

We'll send a simple measurement guide over WhatsApp, or you can share an existing well-fitted garment's measurements — whichever is easier for you.

Do you ship outside India? +

Yes, we ship internationally. Delivery timelines and charges are shared once your order details are confirmed.

About Suitwali

A digital atelier, rooted in tradition

Suitwali began as a way to bring genuine handcrafted Indian couture to clients wherever they are — without a showroom, without pressure, and without ever feeling like a marketplace. Every enquiry becomes a conversation, and every piece is finished by hand for the person wearing it.

We work with a close circle of karigars across embroidery, block printing, zardozi and tailoring, keeping our active edit to a considered 20–30 designs at any time — refreshed as the season changes, rather than an endless catalogue.
